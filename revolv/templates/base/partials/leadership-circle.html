Join the RE-volv Leadership Circle!

Join a select group of clean energy leaders who are supporting RE-volv's mission to bring solar energy to communities across the country.

To join the Leadership Circle, please make a tax-deductible donation of $1,000 or more, or sign up to become a monthly donor for an amount that equals $1,000 or more over the next 12 months.

Your support helps RE-volv staff train clean energy leaders to spearhead a solar energy project for an organization in their community.

Photo below: The 2017-2018 Solar Ambassadors pose at the Marconi Conference Center in Pt. Reyes, California during their four day Leadership Summit, where they learned about solar policy, finance, crowdfunding, community engagement, and project management.

Member Benefits

By making a tax-deductible donation of $1,000 or more to RE-volv, your gift will support our mission to empower people and communities to invest collectively in renewable energy.

As a RE-volv Leadership Circle member, you will receive opportunities to:

  • Network at exclusive social events with other Leadership Circle members

  • Attend quarterly webinars with RE-volv's staff and environmental thought leaders

  • Meet with RE-volv's Board of Directors to get an inside look at the organization and offer insights on our work

  • Join guided tours of RE-volv solar energy system installation sites

  • Get on the roof and help us install a solar energy system!

Specifically, you will enable RE-volv to:

  • Empower people who care about clean energy to lead or support crowdfunding campaigns to help a local nonprofit go solar

  • Mentor 45 college students from seven universities in six states, to become the next generation of clean energy leaders through our Solar Ambassador Program

  • Help nonprofits around the country go solar by investing our revolving fund, the Solar Seed Fund, to pay for the up front costs of their solar energy systems

  • Raise awareness about the benefits of solar energy and connect homeowners to solar providers in their area

Thank you for making our work possible!
